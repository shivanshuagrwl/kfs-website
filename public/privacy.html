Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2025  ·  KIIT Film Society, Bhubaneswar

01

Who We Are

KFS (KIIT Film Society) is a student-run creative collective at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar. This website — kiitfilmsociety.in — is our official home for films, events, screenings, and the community around them.

We are not a commercial entity. We don't sell products, run ads, or profit from your data in any way.

02

What We Collect

We only collect what's necessary to run our events and communicate with you. When you register for an event, we ask for:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any other details specific to the event (e.g. year, branch — if asked)

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

03

How We Use It

Your information is used only for the following:

  • Sending you a confirmation email when you register for an event
  • Occasional updates about KFS events and screenings (you can ask us to stop anytime)
  • Internal headcount and coordination for our events

We will never sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

04

Where It's Stored

Registration data is stored securely on Supabase (a cloud database platform). Emails are sent via Brevo. Both services maintain their own privacy and security standards. Your data is not stored on any local or personal devices of KFS members.

05

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following external services, which may process limited technical data (like your IP or browser type) as part of their normal operation:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (no tracking)
  • Cloudinary — for image hosting
  • Supabase — for data storage
  • Brevo — for email delivery

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any advertising or behavioural tracking tools.

06

Your Rights

You can reach out to us at any time to:

  • Ask what data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from any future emails

Just email us at kfs@kiit.ac.in and we'll sort it out promptly.

07

Changes to This Policy

If we ever meaningfully change how we handle data, we'll update this page and note the new effective date at the top. Since we're a student society, changes are unlikely to be frequent or significant.
