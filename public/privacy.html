Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20 May 2026  ·  KIIT Film Society, Bhubaneswar

01

Who We Are

KFS (KIIT Film Society) is a student-run creative collective operating under the KIIT Student Activity Centre (KSAC) at KIIT University, Bhubaneswar. This website — kiitfilmsociety.in — is our official platform for films, events, screenings, and the community around them.

We are a non-commercial student organisation. We do not sell products, display advertising, or derive any financial benefit from the personal data of our users.

02

Information We Collect

We collect only the information strictly necessary to facilitate event registrations and Society communications. This may include:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Additional event-specific details, where requested in a registration form (such as academic year or branch)

We do not collect payment information, government-issued identification, biometric data, or any other category of sensitive personal information.

03

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through this Website is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Issuing registration confirmations and event-related communications
  • Sending periodic updates regarding KFS events and screenings, which you may opt out of at any time
  • Internal coordination, headcount management, and event logistics
  • Generating anonymised, aggregate usage insights to improve the Website — no individual profiling is conducted

Your personal information will not be sold, licensed, shared, or transferred to any third party for commercial, marketing, or unrelated purposes under any circumstances.

04

Data Storage & Security

All personal data collected through this Website is stored on secured, access-controlled infrastructure. We employ industry-standard security measures — including encryption in transit, access restrictions, and secure credential management — to protect your information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Access to stored data is limited strictly to authorised KFS personnel who require it for the purposes described in this Policy. Your data is not stored on any personal or unmanaged devices belonging to KFS members.

We retain personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 06).

05

Third-Party Services

To operate this Website effectively, we engage reputable third-party service providers for functions including data hosting, email delivery, and media management. These providers operate under their own privacy and security standards and are engaged solely to support KFS's operational requirements.

We do not authorise any third-party provider to use your personal data for their own independent commercial or marketing purposes. We do not integrate any advertising networks, behavioural tracking platforms, or analytics tools that profile individual users.

06

Your Rights

You are entitled to exercise the following rights with respect to your personal data at any time:

  • Request confirmation of whether we hold any data relating to you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our records
  • Withdraw any consent previously provided
  • Opt out of all future email communications from KFS

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us at filmsocietykiit@gmail.com. We will respond to all legitimate requests within a reasonable timeframe.
