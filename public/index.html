Terms & Conditions — Donations

1. Donations are voluntary & non-refundable

All donations are made on a voluntary basis. Once a payment is successfully processed it cannot be reversed, refunded, or transferred under any circumstances.

This applies in all circumstances, including:

  • Change of mind after completing the donation
  • Technical errors on the donor's device, browser, or internet connection
  • Accidental duplicate donations — contact support before making a second payment
  • Anonymous donations — the no-refund policy applies equally
  • Failure to receive a confirmation email (check spam first)

Limited exceptions: Refunds are only considered for duplicate charges caused by a verified server-side error, or if Razorpay confirms the order was never fulfilled. Even then, processing may take 7–21 business days and is entirely controlled by Razorpay.

3. Payment gateway — Razorpay

All payments are processed by Razorpay Payments Pvt. Ltd., an independent third party. We do not store your card number, CVV, or banking credentials. We are not liable for gateway failures, timeouts, or declines. Payment disputes should be directed to support@razorpay.com.

Toggling anonymous hides your name, roll number, and bio from the public donors list. Your email is retained internally for receipt generation only. The no-refund policy still applies.

Non-anonymous donors are featured on the Donors tab for one semester (~6 months) from verified payment. We reserve the right to remove listings that violate community standards, without refund.
