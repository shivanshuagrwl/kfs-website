Community Standards & Language

KFS is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professionally conducted community across all its Digital Channels, events, and activities. All users — including the general public, followers, event attendees, and KFS Members — are expected to engage in a manner consistent with these standards at all times.

Prohibited Language & Expression

The following are strictly prohibited across all KFS Digital Channels, in-person events, and any context where KFS's name, identity, or platforms are involved:

  • The use of profanity, obscene language, or vulgar expressions in comments, captions, direct messages, or any public-facing communication directed at or through KFS platforms
  • Hate speech of any kind, including language that demeans, dehumanises, or incites hatred against individuals or groups on the basis of religion, caste, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, ethnicity, national origin, disability, language, or any other protected characteristic
  • Slurs, derogatory epithets, or language designed to humiliate or degrade
  • Sexually explicit, suggestive, or graphic language in any communication directed at or through KFS platforms
  • Threats — explicit or implied — of violence, retaliation, doxxing, or any form of harm directed at any individual or group
  • Language intended to intimidate, coerce, or silence others
  • Targeted harassment campaigns, pile-ons, or coordinated abusive messaging against any individual
  • Content that trivialises, mocks, or glorifies self-harm, suicide, violence, abuse, or trauma
  • Content designed to incite communal disharmony, religious intolerance, or social division

Harassment & Bullying

KFS does not tolerate harassment of any kind. Harassment includes, but is not limited to, repeated unwanted contact, personal attacks, public shaming, deliberate misgendering, threatening messages, sharing of private information without consent, or any conduct reasonably perceived as hostile, abusive, or designed to cause distress. This applies equally in public comments, direct messages, group communications, and in-person interactions at KFS events.

Criticism & Disagreement

Constructive criticism, honest feedback, and good-faith disagreement regarding KFS content, decisions, or events are welcome and valued. However, critique must remain respectful and directed at ideas, content, or decisions rather than at individuals. Personal attacks, bad-faith provocation, or sustained hostile engagement will be treated as a violation of these community standards regardless of the underlying opinion expressed.

Moderation

KFS reserves the right to remove, hide, or restrict any content — including comments, replies, messages, or posts — that violates these community standards, without prior notice and without being required to provide an explanation. Repeated or egregious violations may result in permanent blocking or banning from KFS Digital Channels, referral to platform moderation, or escalation to university or legal authorities where appropriate.
